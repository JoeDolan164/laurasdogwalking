About Us

Paws & Paths was founded on a simple principle: pets deserve the best care possible. As lifelong animal lovers, we understand the special bond between you and your pet.

Our team is fully insured, trained in pet first aid, and dedicated to providing personalized care for every pet we work with. We treat your furry family members as if they were our own.

We serve neighborhoods throughout the local area, providing reliable and loving care for dogs of all sizes, ages, and energy levels.
